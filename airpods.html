About this item

The Apple AirPods Max are premium over-ear headphones that combine Apple's industry-leading Active Noise Cancellation with an extraordinarily high-fidelity audio experience. Built with a knit mesh canopy, stainless steel headband, and memory foam ear cushions, they offer both exceptional comfort and a refined premium feel.

Content creators will appreciate the studio-quality sound reproduction, perfect for mixing audio or watching reference footage. Students benefit from Transparency Mode, which lets you hear your surroundings when needed, and Spatial Audio brings lectures and presentations to life.

With up to 20 hours of listening time and seamless automatic switching between Apple devices, the AirPods Max fit naturally into the Apple ecosystem that professionals and students rely on daily.
