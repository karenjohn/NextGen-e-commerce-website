About this item

This 65W GaN Fast Charger uses next-generation Gallium Nitride technology to deliver significantly more power in a much smaller body than traditional chargers. At 40% smaller than a conventional 65W brick, it is the ideal charger for students and professionals who need to charge quickly while on the move.

The USB-C port with Power Delivery 3.0 can charge a MacBook Air from 0–50% in just 30 minutes, and fully charges an iPhone 15 in under an hour. Simultaneously, the USB-A port with Quick Charge 3.0 keeps your Android phone or earbuds topped up.

Compatible with 100–240V power supplies worldwide, this charger works in South Africa and internationally — making it the only charger you need to pack for travel. Built-in multi-layer protection keeps your devices completely safe during every charge.
