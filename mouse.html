About this item

This Wireless Ergonomic Mouse is the perfect everyday companion for students working long hours and professionals needing reliable desktop control. The right-hand contoured shape and full rubber grip reduce wrist strain during extended study and work sessions.

The silent click technology reduces button noise by up to 90% — ideal for library use, shared office spaces, or quiet study environments. The 2.4GHz wireless connection delivers a stable, lag-free experience with a range of up to 10 metres from your laptop or desktop.

With up to 12 months of battery life from a single AA battery, you won't need to worry about charging or replacements for a long time. The nano USB receiver tucks neatly inside the mouse for travel, making it easy to carry between home and campus.
