About this item

The Smart Light Bulb Security Camera is a clever 2-in-1 device that functions as both a working LED light bulb and a hidden security camera — no additional wiring or installation required. Simply screw it into any standard E27 light socket and connect to your Wi-Fi.

With 1080p Full HD video, 360° pan-and-tilt control via your smartphone app, and infrared night vision, you can monitor any room at any time of day. Instant motion detection alerts are sent to your phone the moment activity is detected.

The built-in two-way audio lets you speak through the device in real time — ideal for checking in on family, pets, or deliveries. MicroSD and cloud storage options give you flexible recording management without a monthly subscription requirement.
