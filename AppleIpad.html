About this item

The Apple iPad 10th Generation is the most capable entry-level iPad Apple has ever made. Redesigned from the ground up, it features a fully laminated display, the A14 Bionic chip, and a front-facing Ultra Wide camera in landscape orientation — making it perfect for video calls, online classes, and creative work.

Students will love the compatibility with the Magic Keyboard Folio and Apple Pencil, turning the iPad into a versatile productivity device for note-taking, drawing, and writing essays. Content creators benefit from the 12MP cameras and access to professional apps like Procreate and LumaFusion.

With USB-C connectivity, Wi-Fi 6 speeds, and up to 10 hours of battery life, this iPad is built for full days of use — from lectures to late-night streaming.
