About this item

The Rechargeable Smart LED Motion Sensor Light is an all-in-one smart lighting solution that requires no wiring, no electrician, and no ongoing battery purchases. Charge it once via USB-C and enjoy up to six months of motion-activated lighting on a single charge.

It integrates seamlessly with Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant, so you can control it with voice commands or automate it as part of your smart home routine. Set schedules, change colours, adjust brightness, and monitor battery levels — all from your smartphone.

The IP65 water resistance rating means it works just as well outside as it does indoors. Mount it on a wall, under a cabinet, or along a pathway using the included adhesive or magnetic mount — no drilling required.
